Cookie policy

Effective · April 2026

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help the site remember your preferences, keep you signed in, and understand how visitors use it. ArtSignal also uses similar technologies such as localStorage and pixel beacons; for simplicity we refer to all of them as "cookies".

Third parties

Some cookies are set by third parties when you interact with embeds (e.g. an X/Twitter feed) or follow off-site links. We do not control those cookies; please refer to the relevant third-party policies.

Browser controls

You can also block or delete cookies through your browser settings. Note that doing so may break parts of the site. Most browsers also support a "Do Not Track" or Global Privacy Control signal — we honor these when received.
